Virtual Sensory Space

Welcome to this virtual sensory space! This site is designed to meet sensory needs and help regulate emotions. As you navigate through the site, you will have the option to curate your auditory, visual, or tactile senses by choosing to interact with various videos and virtual stim toys.

Please enjoy the site and contact the developer at jadyncleary@gmail.com with any questions, comments, or suggestions. Happy stimming!

Note: This site is best experienced on non-mobile screens that are greater than 700 pixels. Also, minor glitches may be present when using browsers other than safari.
